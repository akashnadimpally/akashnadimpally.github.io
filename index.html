Achievements

The Game Day Analytics Challenge, created by Dr. Chong Oh at the David Eccles School of Business, University of Utah, is an annual competition that focuses on analyzing Twitter tweets related to Super Bowl ads. The challenge aims to extract valuable metrics regarding the performance, influence, and diffusion of these ads. Students participating in the competition gain practical knowledge in various areas of business analytics, including data collection, processing, cleaning, analysis, and visualization. The competition leverages technology such as PHP, MySQL, Python, MongoDB, Twitter API, Alteryx, and Domo to enable students to develop in-demand skills that prepare them for success in the job market. Teams participating in the challenge are required to present the best infographics and white papers that showcase the insights derived from their analysis of the Super Bowl ads. By analyzing social media platforms like Twitter, in addition to traditional mechanisms like the USA Today AD METER ratings, brands can monitor the performance of their Super Bowl ads more comprehensively. These digital footprints provide in-depth understanding of consumer feedback and reactions to the ads, allowing brands to gain valuable insights into their ad campaigns.
The Information Systems Game Day Analytics Challenge 2023 witnessed an exceptional achievement as our team secured the coveted 1st place. Through meticulous analysis of 2 million Super Bowl ad tweets, we utilized the power of the Twitter API to extract invaluable insights on ad sentiment and ROI, offering advertisers a deeper understanding of their campaign performance. Employing a diverse array of cutting-edge technologies including Python, Azure Cognitive Services, Alteryx, and Tableau, we delivered a comprehensive and visually captivating analysis that left a lasting impression. Our team's dedication, expertise, and unwavering commitment to excellence propelled us to this remarkable victory, solidifying our position as leaders in the field of business analytics.
